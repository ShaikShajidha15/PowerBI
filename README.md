**SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT DASHBOARD (POWER BI)**

**Project Overview :**

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of supply chain performance across multiple suppliers, product types, and locations. It enables business stakeholders to monitor cost efficiency, lead time performance, defect rates, and revenue contribution in a single interactive report.
The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making for procurement, operations, and supply chain management teams.

**Key Objectives :**

* Track overall supply chain performance metrics
* Identify best and worst-performing suppliers
* Analyze defect rates and inspection outcomes
* Compare revenue by supplier, product type, and location
* Evaluate cost distribution across cities
* Monitor lead time efficiency

**Key Metrics (KPIs) :**

The dashboard highlights the following KPIs:

* Total Cost – ₹1.73M
* Ordered Quantity – 9,754 units
* Average Lead Time – 28.71 days
* Average Defect Rate
* Daily & Monthly Orders
* Max / Min Cost per Unit
* SKU High & Low Defect Counts

**Dashboard Features :**

**1️⃣ Supplier Performance Analysis**
* SKU count per supplier
